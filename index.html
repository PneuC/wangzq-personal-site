    About Me

    Hello! I am a third year Master's student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), under the supervision of Dr. Jialin Liu. Previously, I received my Bachelor degree from the department of computer science and engineering at southern university of science and technology (SUSTech). My current research primarily focuses on procedural content generation (PCG) via AI techniques, including reinforcement learning, deep neural networks, generative adversarial networks and evolutionary computation. Specifically, I am investigating real-time generation of game levels through reinforcement learning, which involves modelling game level generation tasks into Markov decision processes.

    While my current research is focused on applying various AI techniques in games, I am particularly passionate about reinforcement learning. I'm not content with applying existing techniques, but curious about the underlying principles and theories of the algorithms. Therefore, I am actively applying for PhD programs to delve deeper into reinforcement learning algorithms and theories.

    In my future PhD studies, I aspire to investigate deep reinforcement learning methods that facilitate the discovery of behaviorally diverse policies. However, I maintain a broad interest in topics within the field of deep learning and reinforcement learning.

    Beyond research, I am also an amateur finger-style guitar player. I upload finger-style guitar playing videos to my bilibili channel. Please check it out if you are interested!
